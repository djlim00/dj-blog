## Reading & Writing

1. Use these words to write a logical sentence with a relative clause: city / where
   
   A example : Seoul is the city where <span style="background:#d4b106"> I </span> live.
   해설 : 관계절 문법, where 다음에는 명사가 나와야함.
   
2. In this sentence, what is the meaning of 'xyz'?
   "Although Sujin used to be very _xyz_, she doesn't talk that much anymore."
   
   A : talkative, extroverted... etc
   해설 : used to be(과거에 ~했다) 그렇다는 것은 뒤에 doesn't talk...anymore과 반대되는 성향을 가졌다는 뜻

3. What needs to be changed for this sentence to make sense?
   "The restaurant where make those great pizzas has just closed."
   
   A : The restaurant where <span style="background:#d4b106">they</span> make those great pizzas has just closed.
   해설 : 위에서 언급했듯이 where 다음에는 명사가 나와야함.

4. In this sentence, what is the meaning of 'pqr'?
   "After talking to his ex-girlfriend over drinks, Brent realized that his _pqr_, or stubbornness, was the main reason they broke up."
   
   A : stubborness(attitude)
   해설 : his pqr, or stubborness... 이니까 원인이 되는 두개가 같다는 것을 알 수 있음 따라서 stubborness와 동일한 의미임.

5. In this sentence, what is the meaning of 'ijks'?
   "Most people understand that _ijks_, including kindergartens, high schools, and colleges, requires both government and community support to function efficiently."
   
   A : schools, educational institutions
   해설 : including ... 이라는 것은 그 뒤로는 앞에서 내가 말한 것의 예시가 나온 다는 것.

## Miscellaneous(이것저것)

1. Use a response question to show interest in this statement:
   "My ex-girlfriend was so boring!"
   
   A : Was she?
   해설 : response question은 되물어보는 문자의 동사랑 주어를 다시 되물어보는 것!

2. In this sentence, what does 'them' refer to?
   "X-rays allow art historians to examine paintings internally without damaging _them_."
   
   A : paintings
   해설 : 이건 그냥 문자 보고 해석하는게.. damaging을 받는 명사가 뭔지 보기(them이라는 점에서 복수인 명사를 찾아보는 것도 힌트가 될 수 있겠음 -> 이 문제에서는 뭐 죄다 복수라..)

3. What is the proper response question to this statement:
   "My father has never visited Korea."
   
   A : Hasn't he?
   해설 : 아까 위에서 말했듯이 똑같이 하면 된다. has never -> hasn't.

4. In this sentence, what do 'they' and 'them' refer to?
   "Detergents clean clothes by first removing particles of dirt from the fabric, then suspending <u>them</u> until _<u>they</u> can be washed away."
   
   A : particles of dirt
   해설 : washed away당하는 것이 뭔지 생각하기.
