## Grammer

1. What needs to be changed for this sentence to make sense?
	"Dukbo has watched UFC fights every week <span style="background:#fff88f">for</span> he first saw it when he was a teenager."
	답 : for -> Since
	이유 : 청소년기에 처음 보고난 이후부터~ 라는 시점이 있으므로 Since를 써야함, for을 쓸거면 구체적인 기간이 있어야함.


2. What needs to be changed for this sentence to make sense?
	"I have a lot of problems, but I'm proud that I recovered <span style="background:#fff88f">about</span> my fear of small dog"
	답 : about -> from
	이유 : recover from(~로부터 회복하다)


3. Write the correct form of the verb:
	"Thank God that I survived my English presentation last week. I ------ (feel) so scared since I was a child."
	답 : haven't felt, had't felt
	이유 : "어릴떄부터 해서 이렇게 까지 무서웠던 적은 없었다"라는 뜻이므로, 어릴떄 ~ 지금이니까 have+pp 근데 "~적은 없었다"니까 not 추가.


4. What needs to be changed for this sentence to make sense?
	"Konkuk is a university that <span style="background:#fff88f">it</span> has a lot of international students."
	답 : it 삭제
	이유 : that 다음에 오는 말은 Konkuk에 대한 설명인데, that이 이미 주어 역할을 하고 있으므로 it은 필요없음
